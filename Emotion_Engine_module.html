Tier 1: Necessity
These represent what a pokemon needs on a day-to-day basis. Enough food to last a day, shelter for their daily rest, and healthy enough to continue on the next day. Health here represents two levels of a pokemon's wounds: full and half health. Food represents two meals a day, but this can be adjusted depending on the game being played, if a pokemon is allowed to forage for a block of time, one of the pips may be fulfilled without player interaction. Shelter is anyplace that keeps the pokemon out of the elements. Staying in a pokeball counts for this, but it isn't as good from a narrative perspective as say, sharing a tent with their trainer.

Tier 2: Motivations and Wants
After the necessities have been fulfilled, a pokemon can focus on other needs and wants. Pick one from each catagory and roll 1d6 to determine what the threshold is for each. Goind over the threshold in a single day tires the pokemon out and removes Bonus Dice from leadership checks for every catagory that goes over the threshold..
