Confusion
Those inflicted must make an Average resilience check while attempting an attack. Upon failure, their attack hits a random target. Target can be self, other, or battlefield. The inflicted is cured of this condition if they succeed at a Hard resilience check at the end of the round, or if they leave the battlefield.

Infatuation
Those inflicted must make an Average resilience check when attempting an attack upon targets of the opposite gender. Upon failure, the attack also fails. The inflicted is cured of this condition if they succeed at a Hard resilience check at the end of the round, or if they leave the battlefield.
