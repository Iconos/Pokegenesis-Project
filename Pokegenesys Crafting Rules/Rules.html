Step 2a
Identifying Ingredients
8
This step isn't necessary for basic crafting.
9
A player may identify a number of scraps equal to their Relavant Knowledge Skill +1
10
The relavant knowledge skills are Knowledge:Ancient, Knowledge:Nature, and Knowledge:Tech
11
This means that if a player only has one point in Knowledge:Ancient, two points in Knowledge:Nature, and no points in Knowledge:Tech, they can Identify 2 Ancient Scraps, 3 Nature Scraps, and 1 Tech Scrap in a single watch.
12
Roll the appropriate table for the type of scrap to determine its identity.
13
If the item is elemental in nature, roll again to determine which element the item belongs too.
14
All other items are considered to have all of the properties listed on the table. Later on, one of these properties will be selected from the list for each item used to craft another item.
15
If a specific item is wanted, the player may pay a Story Point and a number of scraps of the same type equal to 5 - Knowledge:(Ancient/Nature/Tech)

Step 2B
Crafting an Item
18
crafting an Item requires one to follow a basic recipe for the item being crafted.
19
When reading the generic recipe, the ">" symbol just means that there are more than the second ingredient listed, with a minimum of 1.
20
Of the required amount of ingredients, Specific Ingredients must be a part of the required amount of ingredients if any Specific Ingredients are listed.
21
Only a number of items equal to a player's Crafting Skill can be crafted per Watch per player.
